At The Grand Dine, we invite you to savor a harmonious blend of culinary artistry, elegance, and sophistication. Here, every dish tells a story, every moment is thoughtfully crafted, and every guest is treated like royalty.

About Us

The Grand Dine is a destination for those who appreciate the finer things in life—a sanctuary for connoisseurs of taste, refinement, and timeless elegance. Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers an intimate ambiance that combines classical opulence with contemporary luxury.

Our Story

The Grand Dine was born from a shared passion for gourmet dining and a vision of creating a sanctuary for epicurean excellence.

The name "The Grand Dine" reflects the founders' philosophy: a dining experience should be grand in every sense of the word, capturing the essence of luxury, warmth, and authenticity. From the intricate detailing of the decor to the impeccable service and world-class cuisine, every element is crafted to transport guests into a world of timeless sophistication and culinary indulgence.

This Month's Courses

At The Grand Dine, we specialize in a curated four course options dining experience that evolves monthly.

The Grand Symphony

Delicate Amuse-bouche, followed by a Cured Red Fish appetizer. The seafood course presents a Golden Yolk with Clams in a velvety cream sauce. Main course, Slow-cooked Beef with Creamy Purée and Tiramisu for a final indulgence.

The Grand Shore

A fresh Seafood Appetizer, scallops served on a bed of garnished shell. Followed by Octopus with Crisp Vegetables. The main course features Slow-cooked Lamb and Hand-made Tortellini in Cream Sauce.

The Grand Vibrance

Grilled Corn and Artichoke on a smooth tomato purée, delicate King Crab with Cucumber and Creamy Sauce. Then, a refreshing Berry Sorbet and finish with a Succulent Beef Filet accompanied by creamy risotto and wild mushrooms.

The Grand Garden

Begin with a Zucchini Blossom in a light tomato broth, followed by a Colorful Vegetable Medley paired with grilled baby corn and purple cauliflower. Shrimp and Zucchini Ribbon Salad. and finish with a variety of House-made Vegetable Crisps
